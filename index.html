Read Rules of Exam First

  1. The exam has 2 levels.
  2. Each level has 5 questions.
  3. Each question has a score.
  4. At the end of the level, your grade is calculated
  5. To move to the next level, you must not score less than 60%.
  6. If you score less than 60%, you will not pass.
  7. You have one attempt to retake the exam if you fail the first time.
  8. If you fail twice, you have to wait to retake it again.
